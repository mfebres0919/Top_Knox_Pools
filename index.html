Questions & Answers

Everything You Need To Know Before You Build

Straight answers to what Knoxville homeowners ask us most — from cost and timeline to permits and warranty.

Call us (865) 335-8047 Get Quote

Every Top Knox pool is custom-built gunite — engineered and shaped on your property, not dropped in as a pre-formed fiberglass shell. That means we can match any size, depth, or shape your yard and vision call for, with the structural durability gunite is known for in East Tennessee soil.

Most custom gunite projects run 8 to 12 weeks from excavation to first swim, depending on design complexity, weather, and any decking or landscaping work. After your site visit we give you a realistic, milestone-based schedule — and we keep you updated at every phase.

Most of our custom gunite builds in the Knoxville area start in the mid five figures and scale with size, depth, finishes, and features like spas, tanning ledges, or water features. Rather than quote a vague ballpark, we price every project after walking your yard — so the number you get reflects your real lot and goals.

Yes. We manage the full permitting process with the City of Knoxville and surrounding municipalities, and we'll prepare the documentation your HOA needs for approval. Handling the paperwork is part of the service — you don't have to chase down a single form.

We build across the greater Knoxville area and East Tennessee — including Farragut, Bearden, West Knoxville, Maryville, Powell, Oak Ridge, and Lenoir City. Not sure if you're in range? Send us your address and we'll confirm right away.

Your pool's gunite shell is backed by a structural warranty, and the equipment we install carries the manufacturers' warranties on top of our own workmanship guarantee. Because we build with our own crew, the people standing behind the work are the same people who poured it.
